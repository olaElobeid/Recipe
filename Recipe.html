My Favorite Recipe: Eggs with Tomato

This simple and delicious dish is my favorite childhood recipe. My mom used to make it for me on cozy weekend mornings, and the aroma always brings back warm memories.

Ingredients | Instructions | Memories

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: pinch of cumin or chili flakes

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until soft and translucent.
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and cook until they soften and release their juices.
  4. Crack the eggs directly into the pan and gently stir them in with the tomato mixture.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and optional spices. Stir until the eggs are cooked to your liking.
  6. Serve hot with fresh bread or pita.

Memories in a Bite

Every time I cook this dish, it reminds me of my mom standing by the stove, smiling and humming softly. This meal is not just food — it's a memory served warm.
